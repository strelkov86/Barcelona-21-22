More than a Club

One Team. One City. One Love

FC Barcelona is the great club with own history. It unites millions of fans around the world. All the best football players want to be here and wear a blue-garnet shirt with pride.

Season 21/22 showed the fans talented young people who are already able to take the game into their own hands and make a difference. Unfortunately, Barcelona was left without trophies this season, but it showed what it is capable of, giving out a series of excellent matches in the middle of the season. Xavi's team will fight for all the titles next season!
